01 About Me

I'm Anay — a second-year CSE student who decided semester breaks are for building, not resting. What started as "let me learn pandas" turned into a full self-taught roadmap: Python foundations, the entire NumPy/Matplotlib/Seaborn stack, statistics, scikit-learn, SQL, and now real Kaggle competitions.

My method is simple and a little stubborn: watch a concept once, then type every line of code from scratch — no copy-paste. Every topic ends in a project, every project gets pushed to GitHub with a proper README, and every morning starts with a blank-notebook revision drill before anything new begins.

Outside of data science, I run on competitive programming — currently 200+ problems deep on LeetCode, with trees and graphs as my strong suit and DP slowly becoming one too.

Right now: deep in Phase 6 of my roadmap — Kaggle EDA and portfolio projects. Just wrapped the Titanic and House Prices competitions, building toward three larger portfolio projects (Indian Agriculture, COVID Dashboard, IPL Analysis) before a capstone.

Up next: Phase 7 — advanced ML with XGBoost and ensembles, a first pass at neural networks with Keras/TensorFlow, and shipping a model with Streamlit so it's actually usable by someone other than me.

I like minimal UI, readable code, and proof over promises — which is why this page is mostly just a list of things I actually finished.
